1.5 Linux embraces the CLI

CLI - You tell the computer what you want to do.
GUI looks neat and colourful
CLI is text based typing

Windows and macos - shield you from touching CLI.
Linux - embraces the CLI for its power and speed.

Once you know the CLI commands in a type of distro (debian for example) it works on all distros of that type.
