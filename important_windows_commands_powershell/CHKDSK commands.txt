/f fixes logical file system errors
chkdsk c: /f 

/r locates bad sectors and recovers readable data (implies /f)
chkdsk c: /r

/x Forces the volume to dismount first (implies /f) – useful when the drive is in use
chkdsk c: /x

/scan Performs an online scan on Windows 10/11 without needing a reboot (read‑only)
chkdsk C: /scan

/spotfix Quick spot‑check and fix (Windows 10+), faster than full /r
chkdsk C: /spotfix

/offlinescanandfix Runs a full scan and fix on an offline volume (requires reboot)
chkdsk C: /offlinescanandfix

/i Performs a less thorough check of index entries (faster) – used with /r
chkdsk c: /r /i

/c 	Skips checking cycles within the folder structure (faster) – used with /r
structure (faster) – used with /r	chkdsk G: /r /c
