About this modpack

The version 1.4.3 of codename purrilion is my current latest public release. It is my most up to date and functional version of the pack on 1.21.5

Some of the things my pack features are distant horizons, a insane amount of optimizations/bugfixes, some hypixel QOL, some eyecandy and broadened support.

Some of the modifications that lie in my pack are the following in these categories

Eyecandy:
Female gender mod to make feminine skins stick out more by adding whats missing.
Distant horizons to add an increased render distance with generally lowered resource usage
A first persosn renderer to render your skin in first person for a better immersion into the game
Shader support via Iris incase you like some well done shadows (given that comes at a performance price)

QOL
A zoom button to be able to tell what things are at far distances
A chat upgrade for a multitude of features. Two being a resizable chat and the ability to copy chat messages.
A dedicated skyblock mod called skyblocker wich gives all sorts of goodies. Some more important things from skyblocker are the following: A dungeon secret solver, experimentation table solver, a repurpose of the recipe book to show all the mayors, items and more along with any recipe that may follow and the list continues.

Optimisations:
A bunch of everything to fix the game performance up to what it should realistically be For example some of the mods include krypton, wich improves your connection stability.
Concurrent chunk management engine wich as the name suggests manages chunks better than the normal one minecraft brings.
Debugify wich brings bug fixes to issues found on the minecraft bug tracker
Disconnect packet fix fixes a few discocnnecting issues to further maintain a better connection
And the list continues for far too long where your attention span from reading all of this dissapears.

This all is for all versions that are 1.4.0+ Older versions do not have some of these things and are on older versions such as not having concurrent chunk management engine.
